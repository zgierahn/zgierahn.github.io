About

I'm a full stack Software Engineer currently based out of Truckee, California.

I am passionate about how things are made and how they connect with other products. My focus is on the efficiency, form, and functionality of my products. This interest transcends both my professional career as well as my personal hobbies. Coming from the beer industry, I was tasked with efficiencies research and production improvements. My passion behind making beer was the ability to blend art and science together

in order to come up with ahigh quality product that the general public loved. I find that software engineering has this exact combination where the backend is very structured and defines how and where things fit together. The frontend however has much more freedom and the ability to do things several different ways. This blend of art and science is how a website comes togther and the reason I enjoy making websites.

Dr. Stein

An E-commerce clone

Recently, I graduated from the App Academy training institute where I learned several different computer languages and worked on multiple full stack projects. My latest project Dr. Stein included cloning the Dr. Squatch wesbite. I used flask, sqlalchemy, react, redux, and javascript to create this site. I also used git/github to track and host my code online as well as render to host the webiste. My primary focuses were to emulate the layout of the site, include a checkout cart, as well as the ability to create, edit, and delete reviews as a logged in user. I am currently working on implementing a user profile, ability to track favorite products, include a search feature, dark mode, and order history.

Discord Clone

An instant messaging social platform

This full stack project was completed with a team of 3 other classmates where we delegated work, collaborated, and problem solved together. We collectvely used flask, sqlalchemy, javascript, react, redux, and websockets to recreate this website. Our main focus in this project was for users to sign up or login and have the ability to join different chat rooms where they could send messages back and forth in real time. This team development was great experience where we were forced to discuss what need to be accomplished, make a plan to reach those goals, adjust that plan as necessary, make multiple different git branches, integrate our work without causing issues, be able to assist and debug each others' code, all within a short deadline.
